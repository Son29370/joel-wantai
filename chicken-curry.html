Joel's Special Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and cook until soft.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white on all sides.
  4. Stir in curry powder and turmeric, coating the chicken evenly.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  6. Add bell pepper and season with salt.
  7. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice.

Nutritional Information (per serving)

Calories: 420 | Protein: 35g | Carbs: 15g | Fat: 25g
